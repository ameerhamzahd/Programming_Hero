My favourite Place

Macca, Saudi Arabia

Makkah (Mecca), located in Saudi Arabia, is the holiest city in Islam and the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). It is home to the Kaaba, situated in The Grand Mosque (Masjid al-Haram), the most sacred site in Islam. Every year, millions of Muslims from around the world travel to Makkah to perform the Hajj pilgrimage, one of the Five Pillars of Islam, and the Umrah pilgrimage. The city holds immense spiritual significance as Muslims worldwide face the Kaaba during their daily prayers. Makkah is a symbol of unity, faith, and devotion for Muslims across the globe.

  • Masjid al-Haram (The Grand Mosque) –

    • The holiest mosque in Islam, housing the Kaaba.

  • Kaaba –

    • The sacred cube-shaped structure and focal point of Islamic prayer.

  • Jabal Thawr (Thawr Mountain) –

    • The mountain where the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) took refuge during the persecution

  • Abraj Al-Bait (Clock Tower) –

    • A towering complex with a shopping mall, luxury hotels, and the Clock Tower Museum.

  • Zamzam Well –

    • The sacred well with miraculous origins, providing holy water to pilgrims.

  • Mount Arafat –

    • The site of the Prophet’s Farewell Sermon and an essential location during Hajj.

  • Muzdalifah and Mina –

    • Important areas for Hajj rituals, including stoning of the pillars.

  • Al-Ma'la Cemetery –

    • Final resting place of many companions and family members of the Prophet.

  • Kiswa Factory –

    • Where the Kaaba’s black silk covering is made annually.

Existing Fact: The Zamzam Well in Makkah has been providing water continuously for over 4,000 years! Discovered during the time of Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham) and his son Ismail (Ishmael), it is considered miraculous as it has never dried up despite serving millions of pilgrims every year. Amazingly, the well’s water level remains consistent regardless of demand, making it a symbol of divine sustenance and blessing.

Chandler Bing

Chandler Bing is a witty and sarcastic character from the iconic TV show Friends. Known for his self-deprecating humor and awkwardness, Chandler works in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration, though even his friends struggle to understand his job. He is best friends with Joey and Ross and later marries Monica, with whom he builds a loving family. Despite his insecurities and troubled childhood, Chandler’s loyalty and humor make him an endearing friend and partner.
